### Patch cable management

**[n]** is short for node. It adds a "node" to the cable and helps to clean up the connections of a patch. Input passes through unchanged. No one likes spaghetti code.

| Inlet | Data Type | Use   |
|:------|:----------|:------|
| 1     | all       | input |
